Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Command Reopen does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

How the app works

The app observes macOS application activation events locally on your Mac so it can help reopen closed or minimized windows when you switch apps with Command+Tab.

Data handling

  • No account is required.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.
  • No user data is sent to external servers.
  • User preferences, such as excluded apps, are stored locally on device.

Permissions

Command Reopen does not require Accessibility, Screen Recording, or other special macOS privacy permissions for its current behavior.
