Command Reopen is a macOS menu bar utility.

How to verify:
1. Launch the app and leave it running in the menu bar.
2. Open an app, close its main window while keeping the app running.
3. Switch away and back with Command+Tab.
4. Command Reopen will attempt to reopen or restore the app window.

Technical notes:
- The app uses standard macOS APIs such as NSWorkspace activation notifications.
- It does not replace the native Command+Tab UI.
- It does not require Accessibility or Screen Recording permission.
- Finder and Dock are explicitly ignored.
